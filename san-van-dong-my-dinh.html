San van dong My Dinh la mot trong nhung san van dong lon va hien dai nhat tai Viet Nam. Duoc khanh thanh vao nam 2003, san van dong nay da tro thanh noi dien ra cac tran dau cua Doi tuyen quoc gia Viet Nam cung nhu cac giai dau quoc te va giai bong da V-League hang nam.



Xem Chi Tiet Bai Viet Tai: san van dong my dinh

Voi suc chua len toi 40.192 cho ngoi, san van dong My Dinh duoc xem la bieu tuong cua bong da Viet Nam va la noi hoi tu cua hang ngan CDV nhiet huyet. Cac tran dau tai day luon thu hut su chu y cua dong dao nguoi ham mo bong da cung nhu cac nha dau tu trong linh vuc the thao.

Thong tin ve san van dong

San van dong My Dinh co dien tich tong the len toi 43,000 m2, bao gom 4 tang voi cac khu vuc khac nhau nhu san choi the thao, khu vuc kinh doanh, cac phong VIP, phong bao chi va phong hop. Khan dai duoc thiet ke theo kieu canh buom voi 2 tang va cac ghe ngoi thoai mai, tao cam giac gan gui va hung thu cho khan gia khi xem tran dau.



Ben canh do, san van dong con co mot he thong den chieu sang hien dai, cho phep cac tran dau dien ra vao ban dem. Ngoai ra, san cung duoc trang bi cac may lanh va he thong thoat nuoc tot, giup dam bao su thoai mai cho khan gia trong suot tran dau.

De dap ung nhu cau cua cac doi bong da quoc te, san van dong My Dinh con co doi ngu nhan vien chuyen nghiep va cac tien ich nhu san tap bong da, phong gym va ho boi de cac doi tuyen co the tap luyen va nghi ngoi tai day.

Cac tran dau noi bat tai san van dong My Dinh

San van dong My Dinh da chung kien nhieu tran dau dinh cao va de lai nhieu ky niem dang nho cho nguoi ham mo bong da. Trong do, co the ke den tran chung ket AFF Cup 2008 giua Doi tuyen Viet Nam va Doi tuyen Thai Lan, noi ma DT Viet Nam da gianh chien thang va lan dau tien vo dich giai dau nay.

Ngoai ra, san van dong My Dinh cung la dia diem to chuc cac tran dau cua doi tuyen U23 Viet Nam tai VCK U23 chau A 2018 va ASIAD 2018. Tran dau giua U23 Viet Nam va U23 Uzbezkistan tai VCK U23 chau A 2018 da gay sot va de lai dau an dac biet trong long nguoi ham mo bong da nuoc nha.

Dia diem va cach den san van dong My Dinh

San van dong My Dinh nam o vi tri trung tam cua quan Nam Tu Liem, Ha Noi, cach trung tam thanh pho khoang 10km va cach san bay Quoc te Noi Bai 30km. Khach du lich co the den day bang xe buyt, taxi hoac xe may.

Neu di bang xe buyt, ban co the su dung cac tuyen so 49, 02, 07, 34, 38 va xuong tai ben xe buyt My Dinh. Tu do, ban co the di bo hoac bat xe taxi de den san van dong trong khoang 10 phut.

Neu di bang taxi hoac xe may, ban co the di theo duong Vo Chi Cong va re vao duong Me Tri, sau do re phai vao duong Le Duc Tho va tiep tuc re phai vao duong Do Duc Duc. San van dong My Dinh se nam ben phai duong. Ban cung co the tim duong di tren cac ung dung ban do nhu Google Maps hay Apple Maps.

Neu co dip den Ha Noi, dung quen ghe tham san van dong nay de cam nhan khong khi soi dong va day cam xuc cua nhung tran dau dinh cao.
