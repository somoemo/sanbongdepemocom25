Vi tri thuan loi

San bong Yen Hoa nam tai vi tri trung tam cua quan Cau Giay, cach trung tam thanh pho chi khoang 8km va cach san bay Noi Bai khoang 25km. Diem den nay rat thuan tien cho nhung nguoi yeu bong da, dac biet la nhung nguoi song va lam viec tai khu vuc quan Cau Giay va cac quan lan can.

Co so vat chat tien nghi

San bong Yen Hoa co dien tich rong len den 8000m2, duoc chia thanh 3 san bong nhan tao voi kich thuoc chuan quoc te va day du thiet bi. Moi san deu co he thong den chieu sang hien dai, giup cho viec choi bong vao ban dem tro nen thuan tien va dam bao an toan cho nguoi choi. Ngoai ra, san bong Yen Hoa con co san co nhan tao va san da bong mini, phu hop cho nhung tran dau nho va cac hoat dong ren luyen the thao.

Khong chi co cac san bong chat luong, san bong Yen Hoa con co cac khu vuc phuc vu tien ich nhu he thong phong thay do, nha ve sinh, khu vuc de xe va quay cung cap do uong va do an nhanh. Doi ngu nhan vien tai day cung rat than thien va chu dao, luon san sang ho tro va giai dap moi thac mac cua khach hang.


Tham Khao Them Tai: Thien duong san bong Yen Hoa - Noi hoi tu nhung nguoi dam me bong da

Dich vu chuyen nghiep

Voi tieu chi mang den cho khach hang nhung tran dau the thao tuyet voi nhat, san bong Yen Hoa luon dam bao chat luong san da va dich vu hoan hao. Cac giai dau va giai thuong thuong nien duoc to chuc tai day cung thu hut duoc su quan tam cua dong dao cac doi bong va cong dong yeu bong da.

Dac biet, san bong Yen Hoa con co cac goi dich vu dac biet cho cac truong hoc, cong ty va cac to chuc cong dong. Khong chi la noi choi bong, day con la noi de tao dung va thuc day su gan ket giua cac thanh vien trong nhom.

Gia ca phai chang

Voi co so vat chat va dich vu chuyen nghiep nhung gia ca tai san bong Yen Hoa lai rat phai chang. Khach hang co the dat san truc tuyen hoac qua dien thoai voi muc gia hop ly va co the tiet kiem hon nua voi cac goi dich vu dac biet cua san bong Yen Hoa.

Dia diem ly tuong cho su kien

Voi khong gian rong rai va cac tien ich day du, san bong Yen Hoa con la noi ly tuong cho cac su kien the thao, nhu giai dau bong da, bong chuyen, cau long, cac buoi hoi thao va team building cua cac cong ty. Khong chi co san da bong, san bong Yen Hoa con co khu vuc ngoai troi rong lon va cac dich vu ho tro de to chuc cac hoat dong ngoai troi.

Tong ket

Den voi san bong Yen Hoa, ban se khong chi duoc tan huong nhung tran dau bong da day soi dong va hap dan ma con duoc trai nghiem nhung dich vu chuyen nghiep va gia ca phai chang. Day se la diem den ly tuong cho nhung nguoi yeu thich bong da va nhung hoat dong the thao ngoai troi.
